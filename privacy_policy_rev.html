Permissions

The following android permissions are enabled from my app on your device:

1) INTERNET permission allows the internet access in order to play the radio streaming, synchronize the data of the app and to submit your reservation request of a track to the radio station.

2) WAKE_LOCK permission allows the mechanism to keep the device on, in order to listen the radio always when the app is up

3)ACCESS_WIFI_STATE permission allows to check if there is internet or not

4)ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE permission allows to check if there is internet or not

5)READ_PHONE_STATE permission is used to submit your reservation request of a track that you want to listen in radio. This request will provide device information to our server such as the device's IMEI number. Our server will use these personal information only to perform your request service correctly and in anyway I will not distribute these information to third party

6)READ_GSERVICES permission allows the integration with Google Manager Services.
